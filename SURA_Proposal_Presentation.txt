[SLIDE 2: Problem Statement]
TITLE: The Challenge of Indoor Positioning Systems (IPS)
BULLET POINTS:
- Global Navigation Satellite Systems (GNSS/GPS) natively fail in indoor environments due to severe signal attenuation.
- Standalone localized modalities possess inherent structural flaws:
  - Wi-Fi (WLAN): Prone to multipath fading, temporal instability, and severe device heterogeneity (antenna variance).
  - Magnetic Field Fingerprinting: Highly stable, but suffers from low spatial discriminability (identical magnetic signatures exist in different corridors).
- Industry demands sub-meter accuracy without the deployment of continuous, expensive physical infrastructure (e.g., BLE Beacons or UWB).

[SLIDE 3: Critical Analysis of Previous Work]
TITLE: Analyzing Standalone Hardware Boundaries
BULLET POINTS:
- Magnetic-Exclusive Topologies: Prior studies leveraging CNNs and Bi-LSTMs natively focus on temporal sequencing. However, relying purely on geomagnetic intensity ($\mu T$) results in profound spatial ambiguity. Our Proof of Concept (PoC) measured a base Mean Absolute Error (MAE) of ~15.38 meters natively due to identical anomalies overlapping in symmetrical corridors.
- Wi-Fi-Exclusive Topologies: Deep Feature Learning via Wi-Fi Access Points (APs) offers better global bounds. Our testing achieved ~5.93m MAE, but purely Wi-Fi arrays suffer fatal degradation when accounting for device heterogeneity (e.g., dropping packets on a Samsung S9+ vs an A8).
- The Private Dataset Paradigm: Landmark frameworks (e.g., "DeepPositioning") fuse these signals but rely exclusively on private datasets, neutralizing the ability to independently benchmark State-of-the-Art (SOTA) claims across academic literature.

[SLIDE 4: Proposed Methodology & Novelty]
TITLE: The Tri-Sensor Hybrid Framework
BULLET POINTS:
- Objective: To engineer a robust, hardware-agnostic pipeline by algorithmically fusing structural matrices evaluated against the public "MagWi Benchmark Dataset."
- The Spatial Fusion Hierarchy (KNN + IMU):
  1. Wi-Fi (RSS): Calculates the Absolute Macro-Bounds (Which room are we in?), resolving Magnetic Ambiguity.
  2. Magnetic Field: Acts as the Micro-Variance Matrix parsing KD-Tree geometries (Where exactly in the room?).
  3. Continuous IMU Kinematics: By integrating Accelerometer and Gyroscope vectors via a Kalman Filter, we constantly enforce a pedestrian dead-reckoning momentum curve.
- Novel Hardware Optimization: IMU arrays prevent logical "teleportation" errors. If a heterogeneous antenna systematically drops Wi-Fi signals, the Kalman Momentum logically rejects anomalous spatial jumps.

[SLIDE 5: The MagWi Benchmark Dataset Structure]
TITLE: MagWi: The Public Standard for Indoor Positioning
BULLET POINTS:
- Dense Granularity: Validated survey grids structured exactly 1 physical meter apart.
- Massive Scale & Structure: Contains thousands of files dynamically organized across 6 distinct buildings (BE Building, CS Engineering, EE, IT, IACT, and COEX Mall).
- Split Modalities: Uniquely divided into "Static" collections (for building absolute truth-maps) and "Continuous Walk" trajectories (to validate dynamic human momentum).
- Extreme Heterogeneity: Tested across 5 different phone arrays (S8, A8, G6, etc.) and 4 diverse users to battle-test gait and antenna variation.
- The Open Standard: Ensures our hybrid architecture can be transparently evaluated and reproduced, overcoming the private-dataset stigma.
